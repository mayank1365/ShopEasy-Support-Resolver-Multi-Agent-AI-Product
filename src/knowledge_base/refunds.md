# Refund & Returns Policy — ShopEasy

**Overview:** How ShopEasy handles refunds, returns, money-back requests, and
reimbursements — eligibility, timelines, damaged items, and approvals. Customers may
phrase this as "I want my money back", "return this", "get a refund", or "cancel and
refund my order".

## Refund eligibility (the 30-day window)
- Customers may request a refund or return within **30 days of the delivery date**.
- The item must be unused and in its original packaging (normal inspection is fine).
- Orders delivered **more than 30 days ago are outside the window** and not eligible
  for a standard refund — offer to escalate to a teammate for a goodwill review.
- **Final-sale and clearance items are not eligible** for refunds or returns, even
  within 30 days.

## Damaged, defective, broken, or wrong item
- If an item **arrived broken, damaged, defective, faulty, or not working**, or the
  **wrong item** was sent, the customer is **always entitled to a full refund or free
  replacement** — this is our error.
- In this case we **also refund shipping** and send a **free prepaid return label**;
  the customer is never charged for the return.

## Refund timelines & method
- Once a return is received and inspected, refunds are processed within **5–7 business days**.
- Refunds go back to the **original payment method** (card, PayPal). We cannot refund
  to a different card, account, or as store credit unless the customer asks.

## Partial refunds
- Partial refunds may apply to items that are opened, lightly used, or missing parts.
- Standard shipping charges are non-refundable **unless** the return is due to our error
  (a damaged or wrong item — see above).

## High-value approval threshold
- Refunds **above $200 require manager (human) approval** before they are processed,
  even when the order is otherwise eligible and inside the 30-day window.
- The agent should confirm eligibility, then tell the customer the refund is approved
  pending a quick manager sign-off.

## Item not delivered yet
- If the order **hasn't arrived / is still in transit**, the customer can't return it
  yet. If it's within **1 hour of ordering**, it can be cancelled for a full refund
  (see Account help). Otherwise, wait for delivery and start a normal return, or contact
  us to stop the shipment.

## How to start a refund
1. The customer provides their **order ID** (e.g. ORD-1001).
2. We verify the order is eligible (within 30 days, not final-sale).
3. A prepaid return label is emailed to the customer.
4. The refund is issued to the original payment method after the item is received.
