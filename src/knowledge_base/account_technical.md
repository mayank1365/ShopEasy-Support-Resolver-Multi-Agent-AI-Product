# Account & Technical Help — ShopEasy

**Overview:** Help with login and sign-in problems, password resets, app/website errors,
order changes and cancellations, and privacy. Customers may phrase this as "I can't log
in", "can't sign in", "reset email never arrives", "the app keeps crashing", "the
checkout page is stuck", or "I want to cancel my order".

## Login / sign-in problems
- **Forgot or reset password:** Use "Reset password" on the login page; the reset link
  is valid for **1 hour**.
- **Reset email never arrives / didn't get the email:** Ask the customer to check spam
  and confirm the email matches the account on file. We can re-trigger the reset email.
- **Account locked:** After **5 failed attempts** the account locks for **30 minutes**
  for security, then unlocks automatically.

## App & website not working
- **Page won't load, cart errors, checkout stuck:** Ask the customer to clear cache or
  try a different browser — most checkout errors are stale-session related.
- **Payment page stuck:** Do **not** retry payment repeatedly (it can create multiple
  authorization holds). Wait 10 minutes and retry once.
- **App crashing or freezing:** Confirm the app is on the latest version; reinstall if
  it persists.

## Changing or cancelling an order
- **Cancellation:** An order can be cancelled for a full refund within **1 hour** of
  being placed. This is the path for an order that hasn't shipped yet.
- **Address or item changes:** Only possible **before** the order ships. After it ships,
  the customer must use the return/refund flow.

## Data & privacy
- Customers can request account deletion under **Settings → Privacy**.
- Support must **never** read out or confirm full payment card numbers or passwords.
