# Billing & Payments — ShopEasy

**Overview:** Help with charges, payments, invoices, receipts, subscriptions, and
coupons. Customers may phrase this as "I was charged twice", "charged but no order",
"wrong amount", "where's my receipt", or "cancel my subscription".

## Accepted payment methods
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.
- We do not accept cash, cheque, or bank transfer.

## Charged twice / double charge / duplicate charge
- A **double charge** is usually a **pending authorization** showing alongside the
  final charge. Pending holds **drop off automatically within 3–5 business days**.
- If **both charges fully settle** (two real charges, not one pending), we refund the
  duplicate to the original payment method.
- Ask the customer to check whether the second charge says "pending" before escalating.

## Charged but no order confirmation
- Usually a **failed order with a temporary authorization hold**; the hold is released
  automatically within **5 business days**. No order was actually placed.

## Wrong amount charged
- Verify discount codes and shipping applied at checkout. If there's a genuine
  discrepancy, we can adjust or refund the difference.

## Invoices & receipts
- Receipts are emailed automatically after purchase.
- A GST/tax invoice can be re-downloaded from **My Orders → Download Invoice**.

## Subscriptions (ShopEasy Plus)
- Billed monthly; cancel anytime. Cancelling **stops the next billing cycle** — there
  is no partial-month refund for the current cycle.

## Coupons & promo codes
- One promo code per order. Codes **cannot be applied retroactively** after an order is
  placed.
