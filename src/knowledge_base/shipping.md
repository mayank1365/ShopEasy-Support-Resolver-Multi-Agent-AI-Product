# Shipping, Delivery & Tracking — ShopEasy

**Overview:** Help with delivery times, shipping costs, tracking, and orders that are
late, lost, or "never arrived". Customers may phrase this as "where is my order",
"my package hasn't come", "it says delivered but I didn't get it", or "how do I track".

## Delivery times
- **Standard shipping:** 3–5 business days.
- **Express shipping:** 1–2 business days.
- Orders placed after 2 PM are processed the next business day.

## Shipping costs
- **Free standard shipping** on orders over **$50**.
- Express shipping is a flat **$12**.

## Tracking my order
- A tracking link is emailed as soon as the order ships.
- Customers can also see tracking under **My Orders** using their order ID.

## Order is late / hasn't arrived / where is my order
- If a package is more than **3 business days late** beyond the estimate, we can open a
  carrier investigation. Reassure the customer and offer to do this.

## Marked delivered but not received
- Ask the customer to check with neighbours, the building reception, and any safe-drop
  location first. If still missing after **48 hours**, we file a lost-package claim.

## International shipping
- Available to select countries; customs/duties are the customer's responsibility.
- International delivery takes **7–21 business days**.
